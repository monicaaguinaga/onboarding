What do I need to do to build an app?

Build an amazing app by following these steps.

Define a goal

Get clear on your why, and what problem your app aims to solve.

Sketch and Wireframe

Get on the drawing board and design the user experience. Create some mockups.

Build and Test

Pick your technology and start building your app. Once you build a working app, test among real users. Get feedack.
